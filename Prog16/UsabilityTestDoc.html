Testing Roommate Ben:

Taks 1: Does the user understand what the Audio Changer site is about?

Result: The user quickly understood what the site was about. The user pointed out that the site seemed simple and straightforward, but mentioned that a better name for the site would be “Audio Player,” or “Mozart Audio Player”.

Task 2: Can the user easily navigate the Audio Changer site?

Result: The user navigated through the site pretty easily, quickly messing around with the different controls, and adding their own titles to the playlist. However, the user was confused on how they were supposed to know what title was going to be removed next but figured it out eventually.


Task 3: Are the interactions with the audio controls simple and straightforward?

Result: The user thought that the audio controls were simple but lacked style and ease of use.

Things to Fix:

Fix 1: Change the name of the site to Audio Player.

Fix 2: Revamp the titles section to be more straightforward for the user.

Fix 3: Improve the style and ease of use of the overall site.
